How it works

One app. Two modes.

From concept to finished draft — ScreenBuddy stays with you through both phases of the writing process, with the same curated roster of writers and directors.

01

Conceptualize

Chat with a writer/director buddy to develop premise, theme, characters, structure, and tone. The buddy as brainstorm partner — before a single line of script is written.

  • Pick one or more buddies for the session
  • Develop premise, theme, characters, structure
  • One-click logline draft from the conversation
  • Save concept (title, logline, characters) into Write
  • Ask buddy to "write out the scene" for screenplay output
02

Write Together

You type a line. The buddy continues. Turn-taking, line-by-line, in proper screenplay format. Your script builds in real time — every exchange is a collaboration.

  • Type a character line or action; buddy responds in kind
  • Suggest next line — buddy offers the next move
  • Undo last exchange; scene list with jump-to-line
  • Import .fountain or .txt scripts to continue
  • Word and page count in the toolbar
