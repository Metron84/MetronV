LinenTable

METRON VENTURES

Curated tables for high-caliber women who shape the UAE.

Verified professional networking that moves beyond LinkedIn — meaningful dinners, lasting connections, and a private Storybook where the conversations continue.

The Problem

Professional networking in the UAE feels transactional and shallow.

Women professionals have significant career momentum but lack safe, high-signal spaces to build genuine peer relationships outside male-dominated environments.

Proof

9.8 million LinkedIn users in the UAE — highest platform penetration in the world

Women represent 46% of the overall workforce and 71% of Emirati professionals

Skilled women make up 45.4% of the total female workforce

Women hold 16.6% of all leadership and managerial positions (over 72,000 senior roles)

The Solution

LinenTable creates small, intentionally curated tables of 6–8 high-caliber women.

It delivers real conversation, career insight, and lasting connection while remaining completely asset-light.

Proof

Strict LinkedIn verification + women-only policy

Tables limited to 6–8 guests for intimacy and psychological safety

Private Storybook turns one dinner into ongoing relationships

Uses existing luxury Dubai venues — zero real-estate overhead

How It Works

LinenTable turns professional intent into curated offline experiences.

It removes friction and builds durable networks that actually move careers forward.

Proof

Verify once with LinkedIn OAuth

Join a table in premium hubs (Marina/JBR, DIFC, Business Bay, Palm Jumeirah, etc.)

Continue the conversation in your private Storybook

Proven model: Timeleft reached €18M ARR and 150k MAUs in 20 months; Chief achieved $1.1B valuation

Who It’s For

Senior professionals, founders, Emirati leaders, and high-achieving women across industries who want meaningful relationships that support both career growth and personal development.
